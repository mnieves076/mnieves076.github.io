Controlling Your Tanks
Player tanks are green. Enemy tanks are red. To command a tank begin by clicking on it. Clicking on a tank selects it, the tank will show a yellow selection box. Order your tanks to move by clicking on the map.

Attacking With Your Tanks
Order your tanks to attack a target by clicking on an enemy unit or structure. A unit will fire only when its readiness bar is at 100%.

Idle Tanks
Player tanks that are neither moving or firing will show an orange dotted selection box.

Starting A Level
When the level begins, units are positioned in their respective start areas on either the east or west side of the map. The level's starting positions are random.

Winning A Battle
The level concludes when one side loses all of their tanks. A window will be displayed allowing the player to replay the level or advance to the next level. The game contains 16 levels and 6 maps.
